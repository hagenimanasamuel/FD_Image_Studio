Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a session with FD Image Studio?

You can easily book a session by contacting us through our website or calling our studio directly. We’ll guide you through the process and schedule a date that works for you.

What is the turnaround time for receiving photos and videos?

Our typical delivery time is 3-4 days, depending on the project. We ensure high-quality results without unnecessary delays.

Do you offer customized packages?

Yes, we offer customizable packages based on your needs and preferences. You can choose the services and add-ons that suit your event.

Can we request specific styles or themes for photoshoots?

Absolutely! We love working with clients to create photoshoots that reflect their vision and style. Just let us know what you have in mind.

What kind of equipment do you use?

We use state-of-the-art cameras, lighting, and editing tools to ensure that all photos and videos are of the highest quality.

Do you travel for events outside of your studio location?

Yes, we're happy to travel to different locations for events. Travel fees may apply depending on the distance.
